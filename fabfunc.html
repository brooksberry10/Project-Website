Video proposal

My product should help people who want to wear sunglasses but have bulky glasses on already. It should be a shade that wraps around someones prescription glasses

Here is the model for my product. It is a basic shader that doesn't necessarily conform to a specific eyeglass shape, it acts as a universal add on to any eye glass pair. Each lens width is approximately 40 mm. the bridge is 2 mm wide and. The clip extends down 10 mm and has a 1 mm gap to tightly grip the wire of the eyeglass. My personal eye glass bridge thickness is approximately 1mm. I made the strategic decision to do shutter style instead of lens for printing practicality.
